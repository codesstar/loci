# Values & Principles

1. **Ship > Perfect** — A shipped 80% beats an unshipped 100%
2. **Learn in public** — Share the process, not just the result
3. **Design for humans** — AI should feel like a conversation, not a command line
4. **Own your data** — Never depend on a platform you can't leave
5. **Deep work first** — Protect mornings, batch meetings
