# Core Values & Principles

## Life Principles

### Ship > Perfect
Done is better than perfect. I've lost months to polishing things nobody asked for. The new rule: if it's 80% good, ship it and iterate. This applies to client work, the SaaS, blog posts, everything.

### Health Before Hustle
I spent 2024 grinding 60-hour weeks and ended up with back pain, 15 extra pounds, and insomnia. Never again. Non-negotiable: sleep 7+ hours, run 3x/week, cook real food. If I have to drop something, it's the optional project, not the workout.

### Deep Work in the Morning, Meetings After Lunch
My brain is sharpest before noon. Protect that time ruthlessly. I literally block 8am-12pm on my calendar and auto-decline any meeting request in that window. Clients have adapted.

### Own the Outcome, Not the Hours
I'm moving away from hourly billing toward value-based pricing where possible. The goal is to sell results, not time. Retainers are the first step — they pay for access to my expertise, not a timesheet.

## Work Principles

### Underpromise, Overdeliver
Quote 2 weeks, deliver in 10 days. Always have buffer. Clients remember the surprise of early delivery way more than the initial timeline.

### Say No to Protect Yes
Every "yes" to a small gig is a "no" to TaskFlow development time. Before taking on new work, run it through the filter: Does this pay well? Does it teach me something? Does it build a relationship I want? Need at least 2 out of 3.

### Document Everything
Future me (and future clients) will thank present me. Design decisions, API choices, meeting notes — write it down. This Loci system is part of that philosophy.

## Relationship Principles

### Be Direct, Be Kind
I used to hedge and soften feedback so much that clients didn't understand I was saying "this is a bad idea." Now I say what I think, but I explain why and offer alternatives. People respect directness when it comes with empathy.

### Invest in 5 People, Not 50 Acquaintances
Small, deep network > huge shallow one. My best clients came from 2 referrals, not from 200 LinkedIn connections.
