# Values & Principles

1. **Ship > Perfect** — A shipped 80% beats an unshipped 100%. I've lost months to perfectionism before. The market doesn't reward polish, it rewards presence.

2. **Own your stack** — Don't build on platforms you can't leave. Self-host where practical. Open source what you can. I left a startup partly because we were locked into a vendor that doubled their prices.

3. **Write it down** — If it's not written, it didn't happen. Documentation is a gift to future-you. Code comments, decision logs, meeting notes — all of it. This is why Loci exists in my life.

4. **Deep work first** — Protect mornings. Batch meetings. Say no to "quick calls." The best code and the best writing happen in uninterrupted blocks. Everything else can wait until 1pm.

5. **Learn in public** — Share the process, not just the result. The blog posts that got the most engagement were the ones where I admitted I was wrong or confused. Vulnerability builds trust faster than expertise.
