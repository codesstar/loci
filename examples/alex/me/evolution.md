# Evolution Timeline

> Historical snapshots of how my thinking has changed. Only read when reviewing personal growth.

### 2026-03-01 | Pricing Mindset
Realized I was undercharging not because the market wouldn't pay more, but because I didn't believe I was worth more. Sarah's comment about her previous designer charging $175 was the wake-up call. Raised to $150 and nobody blinked. The bottleneck was my own self-perception, not the market. Next raise will be easier.

### 2026-02-15 | Builder vs. Designer Identity
Started thinking of myself as a "design-engineer" instead of just a "designer who can code." The distinction matters: a designer who codes is still primarily a designer. A design-engineer is someone who ships products. TaskFlow is the proof point — I'm not just designing it, I'm building it. Updated identity.md to reflect this shift.

### 2026-01-15 | Specialization > Generalism
Decided to stop taking "any client that pays" and focus only on B2B SaaS design work. This means saying no to ~40% of inbound requests, but the remaining work is more fulfilling, better-paying, and strategically valuable for my portfolio. Short-term revenue hit was worth the long-term positioning.

### 2026-01-02 | Starting Loci
Set up this system as a way to externalize my thinking and give AI context about my life. The meta-insight: the act of writing things down forces clarity. Half the value of Loci is the writing, not the reading.
