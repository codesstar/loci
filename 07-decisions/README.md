# 07-decisions — Decision Records

Important decisions with context, options considered, and reasoning.

## File Naming
`YYYY-MM-DD-short-description.md`

## Template Structure
Every decision should include:
1. **Background** — Why this decision was needed
2. **Options** — What was considered
3. **Decision** — What was chosen and why
4. **Follow-up** — Action items resulting from the decision

## Why Record Decisions?
- Future you will forget why you chose X over Y
- Prevents re-litigating the same decision
- Shows your thinking evolution over time
