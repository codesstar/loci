# Decision: Niche Down to AI-Native Design

## Background
I was taking any design client that came my way. Revenue was OK but portfolio was unfocused. Hard to stand out.

## Options
1. **Continue generalist** — More clients, less differentiation
2. **Niche to AI-native design** — Fewer clients, stronger brand

## Decision
Going with Option 2. AI-native design is where the market is heading, and I have genuine interest + early experience here. Short-term revenue hit (~40% fewer projects) but long-term positioning is much stronger.
