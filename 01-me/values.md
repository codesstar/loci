# Values & Decision Principles

## Core Principles
1. Quality > Speed — Never ship work I'm not proud of
2. One project at a time — Finish before starting something new
3. Health first — Sleep and exercise are non-negotiable
4. Learn in public — Share process, not just results
5. Relationships matter — Check in with contacts monthly

## Decision Principles
- New client work: Only take projects aligned with portfolio goals
- Side projects: Max 1 active at a time
- Learning: Dedicate Fridays to skill development
