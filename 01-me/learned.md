# Lessons & Insights

## Design
- Users don't read — they scan. Design for scanning first.
- The best design systems are the ones people actually use.

## Freelancing
- Always get 50% upfront. No exceptions.
- Scope creep kills projects. Document everything in writing.

## Productivity
- Two deep work blocks per day is the sweet spot.
- Context switching is the real productivity killer — batch similar tasks.
